Organic Bulgarian Rose Oil 0.5ml

Manufacturer: Zoya Goes Pretty ®

100% pure and natural rose essential oil

The Organic Bulgarian Rosa Damascena Oil (Rose Otto), obtained by steam distillation has one of the most valuable and distinct fragrances. Rose oil is widely used in the cosmetics and the perfume industry because of its complex molecular composition, healing, beautifying and therapeutic properties as well as its multilayered and deep fragrance.
Our Rose Essential Oil is certified organic according to the strict Austria Bio Garantie standards. Our organic roses are grown without the use of pesticides, herbicides, or any other harmful additives.
We offer our customers one of world’s most exquisite essential oil: steam-distilled certified organic Bulgarian Rose Oil-Rose Otto, a 100% pure, natural oil that does not contain any pesticide or herbicide residues.

How to use:
Dilute in carrier oil or another suitable base, before applying to the skin, or adding to the bathtub.

Precautions:
Make a sensitivity test in the inner part of the elbow, before applying to other areas. If you are pregnant, breastfeeding or have hypersensitive skin, see an aromatherapist first. Keep in dark, cool place, away from direct sunlight and inaccessible for children.

Cosmetic use:

Perfumery:
top to middle note/ fresh, floral-herbaceous with balsamic-woody undertone.
Blends well with: Rose, Pine, Clary sage, Rosemary, Oakmoss, Sandalwood, Ylang-Ylang, all citrus oils.

Directions for using essential oils:
For external use only. All essential oils must be diluted in a vegetable oil or other suitable base and then used on the skin. Pre-made test for sensitivity is recommended - apply the essential oil in the pit of the elbow and wait at least 12 hours for adverse reactions. Direct and intensive inhaling of essential oils is not recommended for longer than a period of 15-20 minutes. This does not apply to oils diffused through vapourisers or aroma lamps. If you are pregnant, breastfeeding or are interested in the use of essential oils for children, it is recommended first to consult an aromatherapist. Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears, and sensitive areas. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place.
